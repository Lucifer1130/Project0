AI and Machine Learing

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are here to stay. Why? Because the majority of the industry verticals are harnessing the power of AI and ML, leveraging them for a better tomorrow, and creating multiple job opportunities in doing so.

Prerequisites

Most machine learning roles will require the use of Python or C/C++ (though Python is often preferred). Background in the theory behind machine learning algorithms and an understanding of how they can be efficiently implemented in terms of both space and time is critical.
